MEET THE TEAM

Care & Company is proud
to have an amazing team of
companions and care workers who
truly love to make a difference
in our clients lives and enable them
to age in place.

Nancy

Caregiver/Community Liaison


Nancy has been employed with Care & Company since June 2020 and has grown very close to
her clients. She feels a sense of joy knowing she’s made a difference in their day, especially
when they tell her how much they look forward to her visits with them. Nancy is our
Community Liaison, so on top of her Caregiving role, she helps with business development
and promoting Care & Company in the community.

Audrey

Caregiver


At a young age, Audrey wanted to be a Social Worker. Before completing her courses, she
moved to Alberta from Ontario and began working in accounting in the Oil industry. She
worked there for 27 years before moving back to Ontario to be with her parents who were both
in Long Term Care. She went back to school and began working in a Nursing home in the food services
industry. Her Mother had worked as a nurse in Long Term Care so upon moving to Victoria in 2017,
Audrey decided to begin working in home care as a companion. She enjoys giving her clients the
independence they deserve while helping them maintain their dignity in aging. She treats her clients
as she would have wanted her parents to be treated.

Dalila

Caregiver


Dalila comes to Care & Company with a background in nursing from Brazil! She finds joy in caring for people
in all walks of life and truly wants to make their lives better. Her calm and caring personality make her
a great caregiver and her patience shows when she has to handle a difficult sitution.

Dawn

Companion


Dawn has been with Care & Company since January 2018 providing high-level companionship. She is kind, fun
loving and has a good sense of humour. Her clients love her and she enjoys bringing joy to their lives.
Dawn loves to take her clients for a walk or drive, spending time learning about their lives and listening to
their stories.

Diana

Health Care Aide


Diana has been working for the Company since 2020 and has been a wonderful addition to the team.
She is a trained health care aide and works with our overnight clients remaining awake in case they
need assistance thorough the evening. Her kind and caring nature make her the perfect caregiver to
assist someone through a bout of confusion or concern.

Tim

Mr. Fix It Handyman Services


Tim is Care & Company’s handy man, providing services to all clients as needed. He previously
worked full time as a house painter and before that for an international charity in their
communications department. Tim enjoys helping people to resolve problems

Vaclav

Health Care Aide


Vaclav provides overnight and hourly supports to our Clients. He loves to work for
individuals with various physical/mental disabilities who need a little extra assistance.
He is empathetic, reliable and a hard worker. He says working in the health care industry
brings him personal satisfaction from helping those in need.
