About Traditions Health

Helping patients have the best quality of life possible surrounded by
the people they love.

Traditions Health was founded in 2008 when Bryan Wolfe and Stuart Young acquired a home health and hospice
company in Bryan, Texas. Founded with a patient-focused and compassion-driven care approach that
emphasized forging strong connections with local communities, Traditions Health grew. In 2011, Traditions
formed a strategic partnership with Dorilton Capital, a private investment firm based in New York City. This
relationship has been instrumental in developing and growing Traditionsâ€™ healthcare footprint from a single
location to over 75 offices across 14 states.



As the company continues to expand across the country, Traditions remains committed to the same values on
which it was founded: focusing on providing patient-focused, compassion-driven care while building strong
relationships in the communities it serves.
