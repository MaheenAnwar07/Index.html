Health. Independence. Quality Outcomes.

Traditions Health brings the healthcare that patients need to the comfort of their own home. Our team of
healthcare professionals has one goal in mind, helping patients regain and maintain their health and
independence. Whether the patient is suffering from a minor illness, recovering from a surgical procedure or a
chronic condition, our clinicians work with the physician, patient, and patientâ€™s loved ones to determine
reasonable goals and develop a plan to meet those goals.


When a patient is diagnosed with a chronic disease, it is important that they receive education regarding how to
manage their disease process. Our team provides education regarding the disease process, sign and symptom
awareness and management, and healthy lifestyle choices to decrease emergency department visits and
hospitalization.

HOME HEALTH SERVCIES

Skilled Nursing


When patients are admitted for home health services, our compassionate team of
Skilled Nurses will provide an initial assessment to understand their needs.
They work very closely with patients and their physician to develop a plan of
care specific to their condition. They also provide education that will help patients
manage their health long term, promoting wellness and independence. Whether patients
need simple monitoring and education or require more advanced care, such as going home
with a ventilator, our nurses provide the care needed in the comfort of the patient's own home.

Physical Therapy

When suffering from an illness or injury, your strength and mobility may decrease or you
may experience pain. Our Physical Therapists will work you to develop a plan to improve your
mobility and decrease pain.They often use therapeutic exercises, stretching, strengthening and
ultrasound or electrical stimulation to promote strength, endurance and comfort.

Speech Therapy

Speech Therapists assist patients who have difficulty communicating or swallowing.
If you have difficulty swallowing, our therapists will determine the least restrictive
diet texture and liquid consistency to help you eat and drink more comfortably. It is
also very important tha you take your medications and difficulty swallowing can lead to
fear of taking your medication. Our therapists will work with you and your physician to
determine the best consistency for your medications to promote good medication and adherence.


Communication is an important part of our everyday life, and it can be very frustrating
for you and those around you when your ability to speak and communicate becomes decreased.
Our Speech Therapists will provide you with the exercises and tools that you need to
communicate as effectively as possible.

Occupational Therapy

Occupational Therapists provide services that assist our patients in returning to their usual
activities, such as bathing, preparing meals, and hosuekeeping. Our therapists will determine
what is important to you and help you and your caregivers develop a plan to meet your goals.
To achieve your goals, the therapist may help you adapt tasks you are performing or your
environment.They encourage self-management to empower you to understand your condition and
develop a plan to reach your health goals..

Home Health Aide

Home Health Aides assist with personal care and activities of daily living during the recovery process.
The care provided may include bathing, grooming, assistance with getting dressed,
linen changes and light meal preparation.
